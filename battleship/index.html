How to Play Battleship

  • Objective: Sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours.
  • Setup: Place your ships on your board. Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).
  • Gameplay: Take turns guessing coordinates on the enemy board. Hit or miss will be revealed.
  • Hits: When you hit a ship, you get to shoot again. When you sink a ship, it's revealed.
  • Win: First player to sink all enemy ships wins!
Use "Random Setup" for quick ship placement.
