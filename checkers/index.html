Checkers

How to Play Checkers

  • Objective: Capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them from moving.
  • Movement: Pieces move diagonally forward to empty squares. Captures are made by jumping over opponent pieces.
  • Kings: Reach the far row to become a king, which can move and capture backward as well.
  • Turns: Players alternate turns. Multiple jumps are allowed if available.
  • Win: The first player to capture all opponent pieces or block all moves wins.
