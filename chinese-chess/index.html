Chinese Chess Rules

Objective

Checkmate the opponent's General (King) by attacking it with no possible escape.

Board Layout

9×10 board with a river dividing the two sides. Each side has a palace (3×3 area) for the General.

Special Rules

  • Flying General: Generals cannot face each other on the same file
  • Palace: General and Advisors cannot leave the palace
  • River: Elephants cannot cross the river
  • Cannon: Must jump over exactly one piece to capture
  • Horse: Can be blocked by adjacent pieces
