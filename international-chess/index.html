Chess Rules

Objective

Checkmate the opponent's King by attacking it with no possible escape.

Special Moves

  • Castling: King and rook move together if neither has moved, no pieces between, not in check
  • En Passant: Pawn captures pawn that just moved two squares
  • Promotion: Pawn reaching last rank becomes Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight
