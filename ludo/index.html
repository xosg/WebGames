Ludo

How to Play Ludo

  • Objective: Move all 4 of your pieces from your base, around the board, and into your home column before your opponents.
  • Start: Roll a 6 to move a piece out of your base. Rolling a 6 gives you another turn.
  • Movement: Move pieces clockwise according to the dice. Land on an opponent to send them back to base.
  • Safe squares (usually marked) cannot be captured. (Some safe spots may not be implemented in this demo.)
  • Home: Enter your home column after a full circuit. You must roll the exact number to finish.
  • Win: First to get all 4 pieces home wins!
  • This version is for 2-4 players (pass-and-play, no AI).
