Nine Men's Morris

How to Play Nine Men's Morris

  • Objective: Form "mills" (three in a row) to remove your opponent's pieces. Reduce them to two pieces or block all moves to win.
  • Setup: Each player has 9 pieces. Players take turns placing pieces on empty points.
  • Phase 1: Place your pieces. If you form a mill, remove an opponent's piece.
  • Phase 2: Move your pieces to adjacent points. Forming a mill lets you remove an opponent's piece.
  • Phase 3: When a player has only 3 pieces left, they can "fly" to any empty point.
  • Win: Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces or block all their moves.
